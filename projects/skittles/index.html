Skittles

Goal: Try to destroy the snake as fast as possible!

You'll see this if there's a problem.

How To Play

Combine 3 or more same colors on the snake to remove them.
Gray food will kill you!
Going off screen will kill you!

Controls:

				
   WASD/Arrows = Up, Left, Down, Right
   Spacebar = Shift Color Backwards
   Shift = Shift Colors Forwards
